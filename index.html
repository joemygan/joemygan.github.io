Joe Mygan

Joe Mygan is an interdisciplinary artist.

Based in Massachusetts, Joe Mygan explores the inner depths of consciousness through sound by melding disparate sources to craft shape-shifting sonic collages. His complex compositions dissect the perception of time, weaving patterns of amorphous samples and synthesis to stimulate an out of body experience.

He has performed at festivals such as Savage Weekend (Chapel Hill, NC), International Noise Conference (Miami, FL), PUFFERS Festival (Providence, RI), Random Gear Festival (Providence, RI), and HeadExplode.Gif (Boston, MA). His solo and collaborative works have been featured in exhibitions and screenings such as Breathing Artifacts (Chattanooga, TN), Becoming Posthuman (Boston, MA), Boston Cyberarts Gallery (Boston, MA) and Inversion 4 (Chapel Hill, NC).

LSDV

For the past 8 years, Joe Mygan has been a part of LSDV, an audio/video performance collaboration with Patrick Chaney. LSDV uses chopped up slices of found VHS tapes to craft layers of mind-melting regurgitations of the television and cinema they use as source material.

Meanings arise from the chance layering of multiple images, sounds repeat out of context. Their improvisational performances consist of live video mixing, looping and sampling disparate fragments to find a balance between the chaotic stream of visual and aural input. The sounds are sourced from the videos, their practice allows for the passage of time to be interrupted and disjointed into multiple dimensions. Jarringly broken narratives are met with the humorous nature of perceiving repetition fold in on itself to create a pathway to the subconscious. Subliminal messages are dissected and torn into rhythmic bits, pulsating in a hallucinatory glow.
